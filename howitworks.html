How it Works

A teacher creates a password protected virtual classroom on their smartphone device or computer. The teacher then uploads class materials (homework, notes, video lectures, audio lectures, etc) into the virtual classroom. The teacher shares the "Classroom Name" and "password" with their students. Teachers and students are able to safely communicate with each other.
